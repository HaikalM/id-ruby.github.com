January Meetup at Bakoel Coffee Jl. Senopati

The first meetup in 2012 meeting of id-ruby will be on Saturday, 21 January 2011 from 1.00pm to 3:30pm. Mohammad Satrio Utomo will give a talk titled "Prying Ruby", and right after that we will have an interesting discussion about development of our beloved website id-ruby.org.

Where

Bakoel Coffee
Jalan Senopati No. 92,
Jakarta Selatan. (Google Maps)

We only have free snacks and coffee for 30 rubyists so it will be first come first serve. See you on Saturday.
